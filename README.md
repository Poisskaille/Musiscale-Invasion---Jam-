## How was I following the theme ?

While the theme was "Scale", I did somehting music-related based on **minor** and **major** scale.

## Goal of the game 

You have to defeat waves of enemies by playing their opposing scale.

- For exemple :

Minor enemy (Blue). With a special chord : **A** **E** **J** **E** **D"**
You have to play the exact same chord but in **Major Scale** (Red)

Chord are generated during the game. Following music most sacred rule.
Some might sound gound, other not.

*** !!! BEWARE !!! ***

Enemies can change their scale one time while your are fighting them.
Making mistake make you lose time on your timer.
